What are HUMAN Cards?

HUMAN Cards let you explore the world from a selected person’s identity, background, category, and personality.

Create your own HUMAN Card as an AI version of you—an AI-powered name card that answers people’s questions from your perspective.

You can add an optional phone number and share your HUMAN Card using its QR code.

Choose or create a HUMAN Card, open it, then take a photo or upload an image to create Camera Perspectives influenced by that HUMAN.
