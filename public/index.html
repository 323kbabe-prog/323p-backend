What are HUMAN Cards?

Each HUMAN Card opens a private room where Image Connect discovers connections through that HUMAN’s personality, objects, places, and perspective.

Choose a HUMAN after adding an image. The image stays alive in the experience while the HUMAN guides the direction of each discovered connection.

You can change HUMAN perspectives without losing the room, image, or earlier conversation.

A shared HUMAN link or QR is a HUMAN-room invitation. Each visitor adds their own image and starts a separate private conversation.
